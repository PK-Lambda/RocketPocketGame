# 🚀 RocketPocket

A pocket-sized space shooter built as a vibe-coding experiment — exploring how far current AI technology can take a solo developer with an idea and a browser.

---

## 🎮 Play It

Open `game.html` in any modern browser. No installation, no dependencies, no build step required.

> **Live demo:** [GitHub Pages link here]

---

## 🕹️ How to Play

| Action | Control |
|---|---|
| Aim | Move your mouse |
| Single shot | Left click |
| Barrage (360°) | Right click *(unlock in Armory first)* |
| Pause | ⏸ button in cockpit |

Survive endless waves of asteroids and enemy ships. Clear each wave to access the **Armory** and spend your points on upgrades before the next wave begins.

---

## ⚙️ Upgrades (Armory)

Unlocked between waves. Spend your score to power up.

| Upgrade | Cost | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| ❤️ Hull Repair | 1,000 | Full health restore |
| ⚡ Speed Boost | 1,000 | +10% bullet speed (stackable) |
| 🔫 Machine Gun | 1,500 | Hold left click to auto-fire |
| 💥 Shotgun | 5,000 | Each shot fires 3 bullets in a spread |
| 🌀 Barrage | 5,000 | Unlocks right-click circle barrage |

---

## 🛸 Features

- **4 playable rockets** — The Viper, Nova Dart, Iron Fang, Ghost Wing — each with a unique design
- **Wave-based difficulty** — enemies get faster and more numerous as you progress, with speed ramp starting at wave 6
- **Two enemy types** — asteroids with comet trails and enemy ships in 5 colour variants
- **Animated main menu** — live starfield with a chase sequence every 60 seconds
- **Cockpit HUD** — hull pips, wave counter, score, personal best, and barrage cooldown ring
- **Armory shop** — upgrade your loadout between every wave
- **Pause screen** — complete with an inspirational Darth Vader quote
- **About page** — with a floating astronaut who waves back at you

---

## 🧱 Tech Stack

Pure vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no frameworks, no libraries, no build tools. Everything lives in a single `game.html` file.
